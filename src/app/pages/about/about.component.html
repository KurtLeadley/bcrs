Bob's Computer
Repair Shop

Bob's Computer Repair Shop was founded in 1997. He started off with simple PC home repair as side hobby. In 2002, after 5 years in service, Bob decided to expand his hobby and start his own business. He started off in what was an old laundry mat. He gutted the insides and renovated. His priority was to make a place where people would feel comfortable dropping off their PCs and Mac to be serviced.

Growing along side technology

Initially, Bob did simple repairs, memory expansion, and operating system installs. As technology progressed, so did he. He learned all he could about hardware and software repair as well as malware and anti-virus protection.

Bob's Awesome Team

As Bob's business grew, he quickly realized that it was no longer a one-person operation. The team at Bob's Computer Repair Shop includes talented individuals from every spectrum of the IT industry.
